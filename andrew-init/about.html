A LITTLE BIT OF INSIGHT

I don't want to be a drummer
I want to be a musician.

This is the mountain I have chosen to climb.

Touring & session drummer available for work

Hello there, my name is Andrew Johansen & welcome to my page. This website will serve as a hub for everything pertaining to my lifelong journey not as an artist, but as a curious creative. In one way or another, here on this website you will be able to find anything you want to know about me.

It is usually in this section of one’s website where normal people write up a clever bio about themselves--either that, or they have someone credible write it for them.

Here’s the thing:
I’m not clever.
Nor do I know anyone credible.

Besides, I don’t care too much for talking about myself anyway. So, if you want to know more about who I am, see what my friends, family and fellow musicians/creatives had to say about me in the tab titled, Blurbs. If you want to know more specifics about me and my life, I am an open book. I will be more than happy to answer any questions that you may have for me. But, I would much rather have a real human conversation with you than have you discover me and my life-story through a paragraph written in the digital world—a paragraph strategically edited over and over again as a means of trying to make myself seem interesting to you.

I will be more than happy to answer any questions that you may have for me, so if you have an interest in taking interest in me, let’s chat. Refer to the Contact pageto see how you can get in touch with me.

I sincerely look forward to hearing from you!
