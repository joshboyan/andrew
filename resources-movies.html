THINGS THAT HELPED ME

MOVIES

We are told to remember the idea, not the man, because a man can fail. He can be caught. He can be killed and forgotten. But a hundred years later an idea can still change the world.

Film gives vision to our creativity. It’s what our thoughts look and sound like. With our daydreams and nightdreams brought to life, film allows one to experience their thoughts and fantasies in the realest possible way. The combination of music and picture is incredibly moving. It’s enough to change the world, not just redecorate it. Whether it was funny, sad, thought provoking or challenging to my beliefs, these are some of the films that have made me most feel something. My hope is that you’ll feel something too.
