TRYING TO BE A MUSICIAN

The ultimate purpose of life is to share with people. Sharing through craft, word and emotion. If I feel like I'm only giving to myself then I don't want to do it. That's pointless and fucking stupid.

It’s not about the notes you play, the notoriety, or the money you get. Community. Team. Relationships. The human interaction. It’s for this reason that I play the drums. With a want to be the loud voice for so many quiet hearts, it’s for this reason that I want to be a musician.
