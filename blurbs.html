WHAT THEY'RE SAYING

These are just some snippits of what people had to say about me. To read the full write-ups that each of these kind folks wrote about me, click above on the number one cause of brainwashing.

No matter where we were playing or whom we were playing for, Andrew simply brought it, every time, no questions asked. He always caught the attention of the audience. I don’t believe we ever left a show without somebody approaching him, asking questions, focused and interested in what he had to say. He always had a big-picture state of mind with a kind bias towards the listener. He understood that music is created for the listener and not for the musician. Andrew’s work ethic exceeds that of anybody else I have ever known. Some people may practice as much as he does, but nobody puts forth as much deliberate and methodical practice. An- drew’s skills reach far beyond just drumming. He taught himself the piano without any formal lessons, learned to record and mix tracks from simply asking questions and researching, and wrote his own scores for film. Beyond his musical talent, Andrew has always been an incredible friend. Because of his impeccable humility, he would never admit his talents to anybody. He never asked for the credit in our band or basked in the attention he drew. But I speak with absolute confidence when I say that I have never met a more talented and dedicated-to-his-craft individual in my life. Andrew knows himself. That may sound strange, but it is not common for somebody to know his or her self inside and out. He understands his place in the world, and he believes 100% that his life’s purpose is to create, share and inspire. I believe in him, and I believe that anybody who has the opportunity to create along side him is in for an experience they will not regret. -Matt L.
Friend/Former Band-Mate
He’s a man that has sacrificed an unspeakable amount of time for the benefit of my music. Andrew has never let me down. Ever. He is one of the most down to earth, focused, and responsible working musicians that I know. He is my absolute first choice to recommend for all drum work that comes to my recording studio, and aside from being an amazing drummer, he’s also a fantastic human being. No ego, no bull, he’s the best team player I’ve ever worked with- Hands down. I’m writing this for one reason and one reason only: to let whomever may read it know, that you have stuck gold. -Chris S.
Friend/Former Band-Mate/Band-Leader

Andrew was very easy to work with and was very reliable. Before recording in the studio, he sent me demos of his drum parts. The first one came a mere two days after I contacted him Along with the demo, he sent a detailed email specifying why he chose to play what he played, organized by sections within the song.
When the day came to record, he had all the parts ready as promised and recorded them all in that day (in a total of 6 hours), including two songs I introduced to him the day of recording. What makes this feat more impressive is that he was practicing his parts while attending college full-time. Andrew’s ability on drums goes much above technicality. He is able to get inside the music. He is a very competent drummer. I believe he can bring all the aforementioned attributes to future work with other musicians. He has my highest recommendation. -Chris M.
